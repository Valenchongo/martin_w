About me

My name is Martín Longás, and anyone who has met me knows that I have a burning passion for strength training. Throughout my life, I have been involved in sports, but at the age of 17, I began weight training, and since then, I have dedicated my life to it. I developed a passion and committed myself to becoming the best athlete I could be before transitioning to coaching others. Now, not only do I continue to improve as an athlete, but I also help others achieve their goals on this weightlifting journey.
